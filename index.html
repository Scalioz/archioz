About Us

Designing Spaces That Define Lives

From intimate residential interiors to landmark commercial complexes, our multidisciplinary team of architects and interior designers brings together architectural rigour, climate-responsive design thinking suited to Chennai's tropical climate, and creative instinct to produce environments that stand the test of time.

Founder & Principal Architect

Ar. Ismath Fathima

Principal Architect, Archioz Design Studio — Architect & Interior Designer in Chennai

Ar. Ismath Fathima is a Chennai-based architect and interior designer with over 12 years of dedicated experience in residential and commercial architecture. An alumna of Sathyabama University, Chennai, she founded Archioz Design Studio with a singular focus: design that is precise, purposeful, and built to be executed without compromise.

Her core design strengths lie in landscape architecture, elevation design, and Islamic architecture — a combination that has shaped Archioz's reputation for facades and outdoor spaces that are as thoughtfully composed as the interiors they frame. Over more than a decade of practice, she has led residential, commercial, and interior design projects across Chennai, each approached with the same uncompromising attention to detail.

Ar. Ismath Fathima is known for her meticulous, detail-driven design process — every project is carried through from initial concept to a complete, construction-ready drawing set, handed over to the client in a form that allows contractors to execute seamlessly, without ambiguity or rework. This precision-first approach is the foundation of Archioz's consistent track record of outstanding client satisfaction across residential villas, apartments, commercial spaces, and interior design projects throughout Chennai.

What We Do

Our Services

Four specialised design verticals, one seamless creative process.

01

Residential Space Design

From single-bedroom flats to sprawling gated communities, we design residential spaces that balance beauty with practicality — crafting homes that feel uniquely yours.

Flats

Smart, efficient layouts that make every square foot count — ideal for compact urban living without compromising on light or flow.

Apartments

Multi-room residential units designed for comfort and resale value, balancing private and shared spaces within the building's footprint.

Villas

Standalone luxury residences with generous proportions, private gardens, and a strong architectural identity that reflects the owner's stature.

Bungalows

Single or double-storey homes with classic proportions, blending traditional charm with contemporary functionality.

Stand-Alone Houses

Custom-built homes designed entirely around your family's needs — from plot orientation to room-by-room planning.

Complexes & Gated Communities

Master-planned residential developments with shared amenities, security, and a cohesive architectural language across every unit.

02

Commercial Space Design

Commercial architecture that commands attention and drives footfall. We design retail, commercial, and mixed-use developments that work as hard as your business does.

Shops

Storefront and interior layouts designed to maximise visibility, walk-in conversion, and brand presence on the street.

Malls

Large-format retail environments planned for footfall flow, tenant mix, and a memorable customer journey from entry to exit.

Commercial Complexes

Mixed-use and multi-tenant commercial buildings designed for efficient circulation, parking, and long-term leasing appeal.

03

Residential Interiors

Interior spaces that reflect your personality and elevate daily living. We curate every element — material, light, texture — to create a home that feels as good as it looks.

Home Interiors

Whole-home interior design covering every room with a consistent material palette and design language.

Living Spaces

Living and dining areas designed for both everyday comfort and entertaining guests with confidence.

Kitchens

Modular and functional kitchen layouts balancing workflow efficiency with a clean, modern aesthetic.

Bedrooms

Restful, well-lit bedroom interiors with thoughtful storage and a calm material palette.

04

Showroom Interiors

Commercial interiors that are designed to convert — from premium showrooms to high-performance office environments. We design spaces that make your brand the experience.

Office Interiors

Workspace design that balances productivity, collaboration zones, and a brand-forward first impression for visitors.

Showroom Design

Product-led showroom layouts that guide customer movement and put your best products centre-stage.

Retail Interiors

In-store interior design optimised for product display, customer flow, and conversion at the point of sale.

Brand Environments

Immersive spatial branding that turns a physical space into a tangible extension of your brand identity.
