About Us

Designing Spaces That Define Lives

Archioz Design Studio is a Chennai-based architecture and interior design firm dedicated to creating spaces that are as functional as they are beautiful. We approach every project as a unique narrative — listening deeply, designing boldly, and delivering with precision.

From intimate residential interiors to landmark commercial complexes, our multidisciplinary team brings together architectural rigour and creative instinct to produce environments that stand the test of time.
